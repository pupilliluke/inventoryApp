Privacy Policy

Warehousing · Last updated: June 28, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how the Warehousing mobile application (“Warehousing,” “the App,” “we,” “us”) collects, uses, and protects information when you use it. By using the App you agree to the practices described below.

Information We Collect

  • Account information. When you sign in with Apple or Google, we receive your name and email address through our authentication provider so we can create and identify your account.
  • Inventory data. Information you enter into the App — such as inventory items, quantities, locations, counts, notes, and related records — is stored so the App can function.
  • Technical data. Basic technical information (such as a unique user identifier) required to keep you signed in and to operate the service.

We do not collect precise location, contacts, photos, health data, or payment card information, and we do not use your data for advertising or cross-app tracking.

How We Use Information

  • To authenticate you and maintain your account.
  • To provide, operate, and improve the App’s inventory features.
  • To secure the service and prevent misuse.

Service Providers

We rely on the following third parties to operate the App. Your data is handled under their respective privacy policies:

  • Clerk — user authentication and account management (clerk.com/legal/privacy).
  • Google Firebase — database and hosting (firebase.google.com/support/privacy).
  • Sign in with Apple and Sign in with Google — sign-in providers you choose.

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We share data only with the service providers listed above as needed to run the App, or where required by law.

Data Retention

We retain your account and inventory data for as long as your account is active. You may request deletion of your account and associated data at any time (see Contact below).

Your Choices

  • You can request access to, correction of, or deletion of your data by contacting us.
  • You can stop all collection by discontinuing use of the App and requesting account deletion.

Children’s Privacy

The App is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from them.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by updating the “Last updated” date above.
